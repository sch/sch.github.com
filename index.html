Projects

Sol LeWitt
Sol LeWitt was the father of "conceptual art", a movement that sold a description of how an artwork should be created, and left the work to the artist who would implement it. This project re-imagines many of the works at Mass MoCA for computers to perform. Github link
Drawing Machine
Plotting has traditionally been done on a cartesian plane with bands that move along the X and Y axis, but a point on a plane can also be calculated from the intersection of two circles. This project lets gravity do a little bit of work to create a drafting machine that can be mounted on a wall. Coming Soon
Walking Machines
Several years of research, prototyping, and iteration on the work of Theo Jansen's Strandbeest machines. Visual simulations of the mechanical walking mechanism, replicas using wood/metal/straws, and general esoterica. Coming Soon
